General Questions

What is this platform, and how does it help me?

This is an AI-powered legal assistant that provides instant legal guidance, helping users understand their rights and legal procedures.

Is this a replacement for a lawyer?

No, this platform provides general legal information and guidance, but it does not replace professional legal advice from a lawyer.

How accurate is the information provided?

The platform provides reliable and updated legal information, but for complex cases, consulting a legal professional is recommended.

Usage & Features

How do I use the chatbot for legal queries?

Simply type your question in the chatbox, and the AI will provide relevant legal information.

What legal topics does this platform cover?

It covers various topics, including family law, labor law, property law, criminal law, consumer rights, and more.

Is the chatbot available 24/7?

Yes, the chatbot is accessible anytime for legal queries.

Can I save my chat history for future reference?

Yes, registered users can access their past queries and chat history.

Account & Accessibility

Do I need to create an account to use this platform?

Basic access is available without registration, but creating an account allows you to save queries and access personalized features.

Is the platform accessible on mobile devices?

Yes, it works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops for convenience.

Can I change the language of the chatbot?

Currently, the platform supports multiple languages for better accessibility.

Legal Aid & Assistance

Does this platform provide direct legal representation?

No, but it can guide you on how to find legal aid services.

Can I get legal documents through this platform?

The platform provides information on how to obtain legal documents but does not generate official legal paperwork.

How can I connect with a lawyer if needed?

The platform provides guidance on legal aid services and connects you with legal professionals if required.
